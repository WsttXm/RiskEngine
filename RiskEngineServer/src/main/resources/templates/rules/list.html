SpEL 表达式示例
表达式说明
overallRiskLevel == 'DEADLY'综合风险等级为DEADLY
overallRiskLevel == 'HIGH' or overallRiskLevel == 'DEADLY'高风险或致命风险
detections.?[detectorName == 'root' and riskLevel == 'HIGH'].size() > 0检测到ROOT(HIGH级别)
detections.?[detectorName == 'hook_framework' and riskLevel != 'SAFE'].size() > 0检测到Hook框架
detections.?[detectorName == 'emulator' and riskLevel != 'SAFE'].size() > 0检测到模拟器
detections.?[detectorName == 'debug' and riskLevel != 'SAFE'].size() > 0检测到调试环境
detections.?[detectorName == 'custom_rom' and riskLevel != 'SAFE'].size() > 0检测到自定义ROM
inconsistentFields.size() > 2超过2个指纹字段不一致
reportCount > 100设备上报次数超过100
